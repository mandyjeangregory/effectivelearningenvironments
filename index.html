Weld RE-4 Classroom Attributes and Indicators, challenge our teachers to have both physical and digital environments that enable appropriate risk-taking, the sharing of ideas, and learning from mistakes. Students and educators should feel safe, comfortable, challenged, and supported in all spaces.

Effective Learning Environments
How might the learning environment you establish for students reflect empathy for how each student learns individually?
 
Environments that support learning both in and out of the brick-and-mortar setting are key components of personalization. Digital learning environments that empower students and teachers to build and complete learning pathways from the location of their choice lead to higher levels of motivation and ownership. Point of need access to physical spaces that are deliberately designed around a wide variety of learning needs enhances the teaching and learning experience for students, staff, and the wider community.
